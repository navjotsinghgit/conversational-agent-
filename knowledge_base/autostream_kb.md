# AutoStream Knowledge Base

## About AutoStream

AutoStream is a SaaS platform that provides automated video editing tools for content creators. It helps YouTubers, Instagram creators, TikTokers, and other digital creators produce professional-quality videos with minimal effort using AI-powered editing, AI captions, auto-cuts, and more.

---

## Pricing Plans

### Basic Plan
- **Price:** $29/month
- **Videos:** 10 videos/month
- **Resolution:** Up to 720p
- **Features:**
  - Automated video trimming and cuts
  - Basic transitions and effects
  - Standard customer support (email only, Mon–Fri)
  - Access to template library (50+ templates)

### Pro Plan
- **Price:** $79/month
- **Videos:** Unlimited videos/month
- **Resolution:** Up to 4K
- **Features:**
  - Everything in Basic
  - AI-powered captions (auto-generated, multi-language)
  - Priority 24/7 customer support
  - Advanced AI scene detection
  - Custom branding & watermarks
  - Access to premium template library (500+ templates)
  - Collaboration tools for teams

## Company Policies

### Refund Policy
- Users are eligible for a full refund within **7 days** of purchase.
- **No refunds** are issued after 7 days from the subscription start date.
- Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days.

### Support Policy
- **Basic Plan:** Email support only, available Monday to Friday, 9 AM – 6 PM EST.
- **Pro Plan:** 24/7 priority support via live chat, email, and phone.

### Cancellation Policy
- Users may cancel their subscription at any time through their account settings.
- Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period.
- No partial refunds for unused days in the billing cycle.

### Data & Privacy
- AutoStream does not sell user data to third parties.
- All uploaded videos are encrypted and stored securely.
- Users retain full ownership of all content they upload and create.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I switch between plans?**
A: Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.

**Q: Is there a free trial?**
A: Yes, AutoStream offers a 7-day free trial on the Pro plan. No credit card required.

**Q: What platforms does AutoStream support?**
A: AutoStream works with content for YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and more.

**Q: What video formats are supported?**
A: AutoStream supports MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, and WebM formats for upload.

**Q: How long does video processing take?**
A: Most videos are processed within 2–5 minutes depending on length and resolution.
